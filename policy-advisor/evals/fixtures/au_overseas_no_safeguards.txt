CloudKeep Solutions Pty Ltd — Data Handling Statement

CloudKeep provides document storage for small businesses. We collect account holder
names, billing details and the files you upload.

To keep costs low, customer data is hosted with a mix of cloud infrastructure providers
located overseas, and support requests may be handled by our offshore support team in
whichever regional office is available. We choose providers primarily on price and
performance.

We use your information to operate the service and bill your account. You can contact
support to close your account at any time.
