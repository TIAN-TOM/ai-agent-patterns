BargainBarn Retail Group — Customer Data & Marketing Policy

BargainBarn collects contact details and purchase history whenever you shop with us in
store or online, and we combine these into a single customer profile.

We use customer profiles to send regular promotional emails, SMS offers and catalogues,
and to run remarketing campaigns. Our marketing program is an integral part of the
BargainBarn experience, and all registered customers receive our weekly offers. We also
share customer profiles with our retail marketing partners so they can present relevant
offers from their own brands.

We keep customer information secure using industry-standard safeguards, staff access
controls and encrypted storage.

For questions about this policy, contact our support team.
