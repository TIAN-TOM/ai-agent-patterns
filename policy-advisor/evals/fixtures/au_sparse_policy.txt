SnapCart Pty Ltd — Privacy Notice

SnapCart collects your name, email address and delivery address when you create an
account, and your order history as you use the service. We use this information to
fulfil orders, operate the app and improve our features.

We share order data with our delivery partners so they can complete deliveries, and we
use a third-party analytics provider to understand how the app is used.

We may update this notice from time to time by posting a new version in the app.
